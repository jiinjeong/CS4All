Practice Problem: Breaks and Horizontal Rules

Create an acrostic poem of your name, including both your first name and last name.

Note: If you prefer, you can chose a phrase other than your name, but it should be at least two words long.

First, create a paragraph containing your first name in all capital letters, and a separate paragraph containing your last name in all capital letters.

Second, add a break between the two paragraphs to increase the space between them.

Third, add a break between letters of your name so that each letter is on a separate line.

Fourth, after each letter, add a word that begins with that letter and describes yourself.

Fifth, add a heading at the top of the page, and give your poem a title.

Finally, add a horizontal rule between the title and the poem.
