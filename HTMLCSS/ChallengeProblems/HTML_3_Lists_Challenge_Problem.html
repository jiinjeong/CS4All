Challenge Problem: Lists

My favorite meal is pizza, salad, and cheescake. My second favorite meal is stir-fry and rice. My third favorite meal is ice cream. Just ice cream.

Create an ordered list of my favorite meals, in order of preference. Each list item should be an unordered list of the different foods in the meal. At the top, add an h2 heading that reads "My Favorite Meals."

TIP: If you get stuck, complete the next lesson (HTML 5), and then come back to this question.
