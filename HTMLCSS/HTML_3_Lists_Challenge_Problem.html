Challenge Problem: Lists

My favorite meal is pizza, salad, and cheescake. My second favorite meal is stir-fry and rice. My third favorite meal is ice cream. Just icecream.

Create an ordered list of my favorite meals, in order of preference. Each list items should be an unordered list of the different foods in the meal. At the top, add an h2 heading that reads "My Favorite Meals."
