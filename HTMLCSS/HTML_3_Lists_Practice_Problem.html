Practice Problem: Lists

Create some lists about yourself!

Include at least one unordered list and at least one ordered list. Here are some ideas:

  • List your favorite movies or books, in order of preference.
  • List a few things that you enjoy doing.
  • List any pets that you have had, in chronological order.
  • List the countries and/or states that you have visited.
  • List your siblings, in order from oldest to youngest.
