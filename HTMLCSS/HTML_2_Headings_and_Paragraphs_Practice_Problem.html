Practice Problem: Headings and Paragraphs

Create a webpage about yourself!

In the main heading, introduce yourself with your name.

In a smaller heading, describe yourself in just a few words, such as "a dedicated athlete" or "forever faithful to her friends and family".

In a paragraph, tell a little bit about yourself. You could describe your interests, favorite things, family, or anything else.

In a blockquote, quote yourself saying something.
